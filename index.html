Why your mental health is important.

Mental health affects how we think, feel and act. It helps to determine how we handle stress, relate to others and make healthy choices.

In today's society mental health problems are still not recognized and accepted enough. Even though many struggle with the symptoms there are just a few people that are diagnosed and currently get treatment.

With Brainteaser you learn more about your diagnosis, your mental health issue and about tips and tricks to deal with it in everyday life. Furthermore you will find contacts of our professionals to contact when you do not find an answer to your questions.

Our goal is to make information about mental health more accessible.
Next to professional psychological and medical treatment, we want to inform you about your diagnosis and how to improve your quality of life.

Brainteaser is a small team of great authentic journalist and editors who struggle with mental health issues themselves. We cooperate with professionals who offer you a high quality of informative material.

Our website gets updated weekly with the newest information all around the topic of mental health.

Check out our free personalied monthly newsletter to stay updated with yor mental health condition.
